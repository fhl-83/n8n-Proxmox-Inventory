## Notes

- **🟢 running** / **⚪ stopped** badges are generated automatically based on container status
- Containers are sorted by status (running first), then alphabetically by hostname
- QEMU VMs are sorted by node, then by VMID
- The `<!-- KNOWN_IPS:[] -->` comment at the bottom is a placeholder for future incremental IP tracking
- CPU and RAM are real-time values captured at the moment the workflow runs
